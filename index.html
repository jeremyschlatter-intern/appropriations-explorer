About This Tool

This tool extracts and organizes line-item spending data from Congressional appropriations committee reports. Each appropriations subcommittee publishes a Comparative Statement of New Budget Authority at the end of its committee report, showing spending for every program and agency. This data has been automatically extracted from those PDF tables and made searchable and downloadable.

  • Click any subcommittee card to view its line items
  • Switch between fiscal years and House/Senate versions using the tabs
  • Search for any program, office, or agency across all reports
  • Export data as CSV for use in spreadsheets

Amounts are in thousands of dollars. Data source: CRS Appropriations Status Table.
